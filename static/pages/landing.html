Takedown Observer
An open dataset of social media content removals
Takedown Observer is an open dataset that documents what content has been removed from the X/Twitter platform in response to takedown orders from various countries.
It aims to build a transparency mechanism for users to study the extent of social media censorship in their country and how governments wield their power to block access to information on the internet.
The dataset currently collects {{totalAccounts}} withheld accounts on X/Twitter from {{totalCountries}} countries and was last updated on {{lastUpdate}}.
